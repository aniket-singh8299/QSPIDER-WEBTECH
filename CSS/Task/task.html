Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and solve problems. AI technology enables computers to perform tasks that typically require human cognition, such as decision-making, language understanding, and pattern recognition. It is used in various applications, from virtual assistants and self-driving cars to predictive analytics and personalized recommendations. AI is divided into subfields like machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision, and continues to evolve, transforming industries like healthcare, finance, and education by enhancing efficiency and innovation.
